date: "2026-07-02"

ingredients:
- 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter (salted)
- 111 grams dark brown sugar (1/2 cup, packed)
- 67 grams granulated sugar (1/3 cup)
- 172 grams all-purpose flour (1 1/3 cups)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 large egg
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
---

### Instructions

- Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
- Melt butter in sauce pan. Gently simmer for 1 min. Take off heat and stir in sugars. Cool for 5 minutes.
- While it's cooling, combine flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl and stir.
- Mix the egg and vanilla into the cooled butter and sugars.
- Combine dry and wet ingredients in bowl.
- Stir in chocolate chips. Let sit for 10-15 minutes (optional: chill in fridge for more stable dough).
- Scoop 2 tablespoon large portions onto baking sheet.
- Bake for 12-13 minutes.

---

### Fieldnotes

Not the greatest cookies you will ever have, but they are solid cookies and easy to make.
I would rate them 4/5 stars.

My first time, I made a single batch which produced 15 cookies.

My second time, I doubled the recipe and made 34 cookies.

Both times, I baked each sheet almost exactly 12 minutes and got very consistent results.

I put the extra dough in the fridge while waiting for the first batches to cook, which resulted in later batches spreading out less because the dough was slightly chilled.
